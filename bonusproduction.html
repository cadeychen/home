· Summary·

The movie And Then There Were None was released on 1945. Based on Agatha Christie's novel. It was directed by René Clair. Casting Barry Fitgerald, Walter Huston, Louis Hayward, Ronald Young, June Duprez, Mischa Auer, C.Aubrey Smith, Judith Anderson, Richard Haydn and Queenie Leonard. This movie kept most of the original plot of Agatha Christie’s novel.But at the very end instead of Vera actually shooting Philip Lombard, she did it as if the real murderer would beleive that he was dead. However only the 1987 Soviet film and the 2015 BBC One miniseries kept the original ending.

· Productions·

This is an American film. The movie was distributed by major studio 20th Century Fox. The Movie was released on October 30th,1945. The movie was approximately 100 minutes long. It had a budget of $ 1million USD and a Box Office of $ 1 million USD.
